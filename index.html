Our Services

We deliver specialized engineering, maintenance, and field support services tailored to the operational demands of the oil & gas, energy, and industrial sectors.

Core Joint Integrity & Technical Services

Flange Management

Complete lifecycle management of flange connections to ensure leak-free, safe, and reliable operations across critical systems.

Our Work Methodology

1. Engineering Review: Evaluation of flange type, gasket, bolting, and torque requirements.

2. Controlled Assembly: Hydraulic torque or tensioning using calibrated equipment.

3. Verification & Inspection: Load verification, leak checks, and quality assurance.

4. Documentation: Complete reporting for traceability and compliance.

Certifications & Standards

We operate in strict compliance with international and industry-recognized standards including ASME PCC-1, API RP 621, ISO 9001, OEM torque & tensioning specifications, and site-specific HSE procedures.
