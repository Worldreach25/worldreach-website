About Us

About Worldreach Global Resources Limited

Leading the industry in flange management and joint integrity solutions, our hydraulic bolt torquing and tensioning services directly address the issue of incorrect bolt load. By ensuring precise preload in bolted joints, we help guarantee critical joint integrity.

Our Work Methodology

A structured, safety-driven approach to every project

1. Engineering Review

Evaluation of flange type, gasket, bolting and torque requirements.

2. Controlled Assembly

Hydraulic torque or tensioning using calibrated equipment.

3. Verification & Inspection

Load verification, leak checks and quality assurance.

4. Documentation

Complete reporting for traceability and compliance.

Our Services

Comprehensive Solutions

At Worldreach Global Resources Limited, we deliver end-to-end flange management solutions customized to meet your operational needs. Our expertise combines both hydraulic bolt torquing and hydraulic bolt tensioning—two proven techniques for achieving precise, leak-free joint integrity. Whether optimizing for consistency or tackling large-diameter applications, our approach ensures safe, reliable, and efficient performance across critical systems.

Flange Management

Complete lifecycle management of critical flanges including assembly, maintenance, and monitoring to ensure leak-free operations.

  • Torque specifications
  • Gasket selection
  • Quality assurance
  • Hot-Bolting
  • Flange Facing
